    About Us

    Payam (പായം) Gram Panchayat is a Rural Local Body in Iritty Panchayat Samiti part of Kannur Zila Parishad. There are total 2 Villages under Payam Gram Panchayat jurisdiction. Gram Panchayat Iritty is further divided into 18 Wards. Gram Panchayat Iritty has total 16 elected members by people.
